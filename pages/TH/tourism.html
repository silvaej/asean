     Bangkok is a bustling city bringing you the best of Thailand. This enigmatic capital offers a wide array of hard-to-miss experiences and beautiful Bangkok Thailand tourist spots when it comes to arts, history, culture, nightlife, and of course, shopping! Places to visit : The Grand Palace, Damnoen Saduak floating market, Wat Pho, Chinatown (Yaowarat), Chatuchak Weekend Market,Jim Thompson’s House, and Wat Arun.




     If you want to make the most of Thailand’s best islands and beaches, Phuket is the best tourist spot in Thailand to visit. Every water-lover will love Phuket as it boasts some of the country’s most amazing islands. But it’s all beaches though. In Phuket, you can also go hiking, snorkeling, fishing, jet skiing, and more. Places to visit : The Grand Palace, Damnoen Saduak floating market, Wat Pho, Chinatown (Yaowarat), Chatuchak Weekend Market, Jim Thompson’s House, and Wat Arun.




     When we say you’ll see a temple in every corner in Chiang Mai, we mean it. In fact, there’s over 500 temples in this city so you’ll get plenty of snaps and history knowledge when you leave. But Chiang Mai tourist spots are not just about temples. They also have Chiang Mai Night Zoo that will certainly entertain both kids and adults. Places to visit : Phang Nga Bay, Phi Phi Islands, Old Phuket Town, James Bond Island, Patong Beach, Kata Noi Beach, and Karon View Point.




     If you’re a fan of history and all things art, you’ll definitely enjoy one of Thailand’s ancient cities—Ayutthaya. This UNESCO-listed tourist attraction in Thailand will take on a journey back in time to Siam’s golden age through its temples. Seriously, it’s like you’re walking in an open-air museum when you’re here. Places to visit : Wat Chai Wattanaram, Bang Pa-In Summer Palace, Chao Sam Phraya National Museum, Chan Kasem Palace, Krirk Yoonpan’s Million Toys Museum, Wat Mahathat, and Wat Phra Si Sanphet.




     When in Pattaya, expect an abundance of cultural extravaganzas and ladyboy shows, island trips and excursions that will give you unforgettable experiences. Among the shows you must not miss is the world-famous Tiffany’s Ladyboy Cabaret. Pattaya will certainly bring out the adventurer in you. This is definitely one of the must-visit Thailand tourist places. Places to visit : The Sanctuary of Truth,Jomtien Beach, Pattaya Floating Market, Mini Siam, Pattaya Walking Street, and Pattaya Viewpoint.




     Known to be the home of stunning coastal scenery with limestone karsts jutting out of the Andaman Sea, and the most surreal sunsets, Krabi is definitely a must see. It boasts many natural wonders both in land and in sea. No wonder it’s one of the most famous tourist spots in Thailand. Places to visit : Tiger Cave – Wat Tham Sua, Khao, Khanab Nam, Krabi Emerald Pond, Krabi Hot Spring, Krabi Riverside & Thara Park, and Khao Ngon Nak Viewpoint.




     If you’re asking the beautiful places in Thailand, Phi Phi Islands would always be on the list. Even Phi Phi is just a small island, there are tons of breathtaking spots and fun things to do here. From exploring the pristines beaches in Phi Phi Islands to diving deep to see beyond its clear waters, you will never get bored here. Places to visit : Ko Phi Phi Lee, Ton Sai Beach, Loh Dalum, Bamboo Island, and Monkey Beach.
